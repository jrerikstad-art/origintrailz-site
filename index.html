How it works

Walk. Reveal. Remember.

No setup, no routes to plan, no buttons to press. Origintrailz quietly turns every walk into territory on a map that is entirely, permanently yours.

👣

Walk anywhere

The fog lifts in a 20-metre circle around you. Streets, alleys, forest trails — every path counts.

🗺️

Your map grows

Revealed territory stays revealed. Over months it becomes the shape of your life in every city.

🕯️

Look back, anytime

"Have I been down this street before?" Now you'll always know — even from that trip years ago.

Built for the long walk

Quiet, precise, and entirely offline.

📡

Works with zero signal

GPS needs no network. Cache the map before a hike and the fog lifts on any mountain, at any DNT cabin, in full airplane mode.

🎯

Earned, not given

A tight 20-metre reveal radius means driving past doesn't count. Every revealed street is one you actually walked.

📥

Bring your history with you

Import GPX files from Strava, Garmin or any tracker — and watch years of your walking life appear on day one.

🔋

Gentle on your battery

Smart GPS sampling tracks all day in your pocket. Screen off, fog still lifting.

Privacy

Your map never leaves your hands.

✓

On your device by default. Your location history is stored locally, full stop. No account needed, no server involved.

✓

No ads, no data sales. Ever. You are the customer, not the product. One price, paid once.

✓

Backup on your terms. Export an open JSON file to your own storage — or opt into encrypted cloud backup. Both optional.

✓

Campaigns can't see your map. Opt-in experiences only ever learn "this device checked in here" — never where you've been.

We never know where you've been."
